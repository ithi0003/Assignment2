Hotspot Intensity and Distribution

About the Map

This interactive map visualises satellite-detected fire hotspots across Australia. Colour encodes Fire Radiative Power (FRP); size corresponds to scan width.

  • Clusters appear along northern and eastern regions.
  • Higher FRP where drought persists.
  • Hover for brightness and energy.

Forest Area vs Burnt Area

About the Chart

Each bar shows a state's total native forest area with the burnt portion stacked inside it. Use the dropdown to switch forest tenure.

  • NSW & VIC had the largest absolute losses.
  • WA & QLD have low burnt percentages relative to size.
  • ACT has the highest proportion burnt relative to area.
  • Nationally, ~6% of native forests were affected.

Legend: Teal = unburnt, Red = burnt.
